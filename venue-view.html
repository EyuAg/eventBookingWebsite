Sheraton Addis, a Luxury Collection Hotel

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (24)
Address: Taitu St, Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.
Capacity: 500+
Price: ETB 15,000.00/hr

More than just a hotel, the Sheraton Addis is an iconic landmark and the city's premier luxury venue for high-society weddings, corporate galas, and international meetings. It offers multiple, beautifully appointed ballrooms (like the Menelik and Ras Ballrooms), extensive gardens, and world-class service. Its opulent setting is synonymous with elite events.

Amenities: Air Conditioning, WiFi, Parking, Projector, Catering Kitchen, Dance Floor, Stage, Sound System, Lighting, Restrooms, Dressing Rooms
